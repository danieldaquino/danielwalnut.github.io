My Website

BY DANIEL D'AQUINO

I started this website with the purpose of sharing my work and my passion with other people. It is also a place where I can keep progress of my own work.

The logo

The Final logo has both curves and sharp edges, creating a balance between the symbology of high tech and more liberal art. It emphasizes the first letter of my name. It is red to symbolize passion.


Inspiration, color palette and fonts

I wanted two things to set the theme for my website:

  • The dichotomy of Tech and Art
  • Minimalism

So, for my fonts, I chose one serif font, which is very artsy, and one slab serif font, which has sharp edges, giving it a more high-tech look. For my colour palette, I chose three:

  • White
  • Gray
  • Red

These colours are inspired by Zen Buddhist Japanese calligraphy, which is generally done with a black-gray ink on a white piece of paper, as generally also has the signature stamp, which is red. Zen Buddhism is one of the philosophies associated with minimalism.

Also, gray and white show posture and simplicity, while red shows passion.
