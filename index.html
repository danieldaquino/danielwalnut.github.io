Welcome to Daniel Walnut

You come into your bedroom. You're welcomed by the smart systems, as they prepare the room for your usual preferences. You smile as magic happens. You put the BCI device, and the room automatically change.

The preferences look a bit different today. The system *knows* what you wish for. The system notices you're a bit sad. In such a deep connection with technology, the assistant sets quiet, as you think about life, and what happened yesterday. It opens up the windows, changes some colors so everything is more peaceful.

After a while, It asks if you want to play some music. You agree and now you are able to let go of the pain a bit. It tells that your favorite team will play soon. It knows you love that team, that sport, so maybe that will cheer you up, right? Who says technology can't try to cheer you up?"
